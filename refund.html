Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last updated: 13 March 2026

Thank you for subscribing to Daily Brief. We offer a simple cancellation and refund policy.

Cancellation

You may cancel your subscription to Daily Brief at any time. When you cancel, your access to the Service will continue until the end of the current billing cycle. No further payments will be charged after your cancellation.

Refunds

Due to the immediate access and digital nature of the daily summaries, we do not offer refunds on subscription payments that have already been processed. A refund will not be issued for any unused portion of your current monthly subscription.

If you believe there has been an error in billing, please contact us immediately at sanil25jain@gmail.com and we will work to resolve the issue.
